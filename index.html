Our story

Few words about us

Hello Restaurant Companies - Staff recruitment is harder than ever and we know everyone is looking for the magic answer. Placing a free post on this board is not the magic answer. RestaurantCareers is managed by R&R a recruitment marketing firm specializing in the restaurant industry. Please see below our list of things you should do for staffing.

"Housekeeping – Provide as many avenues as possible to connect with applicants who already are fans of your brand. •Add a job button on the company locations page •Review drop off rate and difficulty in online app and assessment process – can you simplify? •Host open interviews – all the time •Review and increase in-store signage •Promote increased referral bonuses – too good to pass up. •Line and Server – what do applicants want from these jobs and - what of these – do you offer? •Explore new job titles that funnel into jobs that require little to no qualifications - host, busser, dishwasher– try jobs like Clerk, Cashier, Counter Server"
